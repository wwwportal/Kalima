

To search for an ayah:
	search surah_number:ayah_number 
	
To inspect the linguistic details of an ayah:
	if already focused on an linguistic unit and want to see all its available linguistic details:
		inspect
	else to inspect a specific linguistic dimension of the linguistic unit {
		inspect <linguistic dimension> |
	}
	else
		inspect surah_number:ayah_number
		
To change perspectives/recursion/layers/scales by zooming in or out, from an ayah to a sentence to a word to the word's morphological components.
To see a Surah:
	see chapter <chapter_number>
To see an ayah:
	see verse <verse_number>
To see a sentence:
	see sentence <sentence_number>
// Caution! A sentence can sometimes be split across two ayat.
To see a word:
	see word <word_number>
// Caution! Two words can sometimes be concatenated into a single word.)
// Question: Can a word sometimes be split into two separate words?
To see a morpheme:
	see morpheme <morpheme_letter>
To see a letter:
	see letter <letter_number>	

(accept also letters written in letters, i.e., one, two, three, two hundred fifty three, etc)
